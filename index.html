CV Description

I believe in the balance of mechanics and story-telling, I strive to create games that are not only fun to play but also provide a meaningful and memorable experience for the player. I have experience in designing game mechanics such as combat systems, progression systems, and puzzles and also have experience in writing and creating characters, plot, and dialogue. I am particularly interested in creating narrative-driven games that focus on world-building and player choice. My goal is to continue to push the boundaries of what is possible in the game design industry and to create truly unforgettable games that leave a lasting impact on the players.
